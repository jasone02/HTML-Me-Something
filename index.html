This World I'm Building

I'm building a world in a fantasy setting for a game I'm slowly making. The simple premise is that mana, which is the energy behind magical power, comes exclusively from the bodies of the recently deceased. The inhabitants of this world react to that in different ways and create the factions and regions listed below.

The Warlike Faction

  • Want to gather mana by killing/conquering/enslaving the neighboring regions.
  • Consist of a small nobility and large slave caste.
  • Almost every noble intends to eventually take over the whole power strucuture.
  • Nobles' servants hope to be rewarded when their masters 'inevitably' take control of this region.

The Borderland

  • Borders the Warlike Faction.
  • More of a simple region than a faction.
  • Mostly wasteland from constant raiding.
  • Most of the population lives underground or in portable cities sold by the Mercantile Faction.
  • Inhabitants are hostile to outsiders, and guerilla-type attacks make passing through this region difficult.

The Pastureland

  • Large area filled with grazing land.
  • Livestock raised here to be slaughtered for mana.
  • Acts as a "heartland".
  • Not a rich region, but certainly not poor.

The Academic Faction

  • A society that has come together to study magic.
  • Funded and protected by The Mercantile Faction in exchange for new ideas to monetize.

The Mercantile Faction

  • Tries to gain power through financial domination.
  • Buys from and sells to all factions.
